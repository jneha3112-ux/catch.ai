Connect Your Clinic

Follow the 3 steps below to link your phone, WhatsApp, and calendar. Once connected, Catch.ai runs fully automatically — no staff action needed.

01

Connect Your Phone Line

Via Twilio

When a patient call goes unanswered, Twilio sends the signal to Catch.ai. Paste your unique webhook URL into the "Fallback URL" field in your Twilio Phone Number settings.

Your Unique Webhook URL

https://catch-ai.onrender.com/voice

How to set this up:

  1. 1. Go to console.twilio.com → Phone Numbers → Active Numbers
  2. 2. Click your clinic's phone number
  3. 3. Under "Voice & Fax" → "A Call Comes In" → set it to Webhook and paste the URL above
  4. 4. Click Save. That's it — Catch.ai now catches every missed call.
02

Connect WhatsApp

Auto Follow-Up

After a missed call, Catch.ai sends an instant WhatsApp message to the patient. Connect your Twilio WhatsApp Sandbox or your approved Business number using the webhook below.

WhatsApp Webhook URL

https://catch-ai.onrender.com/whatsapp

How to set this up:

  1. 1. Go to Twilio WhatsApp Sandbox
  2. 2. Under "When a message comes in" → paste the WhatsApp Webhook URL above
  3. 3. Save. Your patients will now receive instant WhatsApp replies on missed calls.
03

Connect Your Calendar

Pro Feature

Give Catch.ai read access to your Google Calendar so it knows your real-time availability. The AI will only book patients into slots that are genuinely open.

Your Calendar ID

How to find your Calendar ID:

  1. 1. Open Google Calendar
  2. 2. Click the 3 dots next to your calendar name → Settings
  3. 3. Scroll down to "Calendar ID" — it looks like an email address
  4. 4. Paste it above and hit Save
